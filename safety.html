Here are a few tips to help keep you alive by minimizing your Dihydrogen Monoxide (DHMO) exposure:

  1. AVOID SKIN CONTACT AND DO NOT INGEST DHMO:

    DHMO is a colorless, odorless, chemical compound which can be fatal if dermal contact is made or ingested.

  2. WATCH OUT FOR YOUR SAFETY:

    Be sure to always remain hyper-vigilant of your surroundings and be on the lookout major bodies of water and utilities facilities. Also, be aware of water fountains, fire hydrants and any other sources of water in your nearby vicinity.

  3. ALWAYS DON YOUR PERSONNEL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT (PPE):

    Know what kind of protection you'll require before braving out into the open world. Check your local weather reports and always be prepared for an Aerial Aqua Assault.
